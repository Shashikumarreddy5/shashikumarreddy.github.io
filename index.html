Frequently Asked Questions

Can I claim HRA in the New Tax Regime?

No, the New Tax Regime does not allow HRA exemptions. However, you can claim Employer NPS contributions under Section 80CCD(2).

How is PF calculated?

You can choose between a fixed ₹1,800 per month contribution or 12% of your gross salary (capped at ₹1.5L annually).

What is the standard deduction for FY 2025-26?

The standard deduction is ₹75,000 for the New Regime and ₹50,000 for the Old Regime in FY 2025-26.
